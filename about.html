About MaliyetHesap

Free. Instant. No signup required.

What We Do

MaliyetHesap is a powerful online platform specifically designed to help individuals and companies in Turkey estimate construction costs with precision. Using up-to-date market data and localized price indexes, our tool provides an itemized breakdown of building expenses—from structural work to fine finishes—completely for free.

Why We Built This

Most financial tools are locked behind paywalls or require creating an account just to see a number. We believe useful tools should be free and instant for everyone. Navigating the complexities of construction economics shouldn't be a privilege, but a basic resource for everyone building a future in Turkey.
